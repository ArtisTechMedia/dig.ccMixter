Free Music is Our Gift to the World

From all around the world, artists come to ccMixter.org to collaborate -- Creative Commons licenses make our open-source-music interactive. ccMixter is a positive environment where both professionals and hobbyists are connected through music. Help us grow open and free!

How YOU Can Participate:


1. Show Us Your Gear!

How do you record your ccMixter tracks? Upload a picture or video of your instruments, remixing gear, or a screenshot of your VSTs - and an anonymous donor will give $1 to ccMixter! For tracking, tag us on Twitter, Google Plus, YouTube or Instagram, post to our Facebook, and use the hastags #ccMixter and #MusicConnectsUs. Please also post a link in our Forums so we can all easily find what you share.


2. Record, Remix

Contribute a pell, sample or remix on how Music Connects Us at ccMixter.org. Use the suggested tag Music_Connects_Us on your upload. Check out the dozens of #MusicConnectsUs promos and remixes already created HERE for inspiration. Oh, and if you remix Sackjo22, she will donate $5 to our IndieGoGo Campaign for each mix (wow).


3. Follow, Tag, Retweet, Like

Help spread the word about ccMixter and the unique ways we create and share our music. Follow and share the magic of ccMixter on Twitter, Google Plus, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Vimeo -- and use the hashtags #ccMixter and #MusicConnectsUs and link to our IndieGoGo campaign - https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ccmixter-open-music-for-the-world/


4. Vid & URL Sharing

ccMixter just started our official YouTube and Vimeo channels. We'll be posting new #MusicConnectsUs vids weekly. Oh, and there are simple ways to share our IndieGogo campaign URL: Add the link to your song page - igg.me/at/ccmixter, post on your social media, and email your friends that appreciate and support your music.
